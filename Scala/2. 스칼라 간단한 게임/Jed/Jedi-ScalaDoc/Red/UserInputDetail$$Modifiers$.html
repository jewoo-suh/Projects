  •  object UserInputDetail

    A bag of tools to support the simple and intelligible decoding of MouseButtonEvents and KeyEvents generated by the underlying platform, as well as the UserInputDetails into which they are translated by InputPanels and their descendants.

    A bag of tools to support the simple and intelligible decoding of MouseButtonEvents and KeyEvents generated by the underlying platform, as well as the UserInputDetails into which they are translated by InputPanels and their descendants.

    The "little language" of modifier detail implemented here reduces (to zero) the number of imports needed elsewhere from modules defined in javax.swing. A typical use, during the decoding of an event with a mods: Detail field might be

    mods.hasButton1 || mods.hasMeta  || mods.hasControl

    or its equivalent

    mods.hasAny(Buttons.Button1|Buttons.Meta|Buttons.Control)
