Companion object TextBuffer

class TextBuffer extends Sequence[Char] with CharSequence

An extensible editable character sequence

Represents 

chars: Seq[Char]

Representation Invariant

length(chars) = super.elements.length
chars(i)      = super.elements(i)

Copyright (C) B.A. Sufrin, 2021 and J.M. Spivey. 2015

The present module is a component of a refactoring and a redesign of the Text component of Mike Spivey's "ewoks" editor. (Copyright (c) J.M.Spivey 2015) whose inspiration I gratefully acknowledge. B.A. Sufrin, Oxford, Michaelmas 2021.
