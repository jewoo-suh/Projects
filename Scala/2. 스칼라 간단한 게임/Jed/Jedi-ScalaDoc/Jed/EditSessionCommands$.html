  9.  def dragCursor(row: Int, col: Int): SessionCommand

    Dragging the cursor is unusual.

    Dragging the cursor is unusual. There is no need for a history item, because a drag will always be preceded by a setCursorAndMark, whose undo method will suffice to undo the whole press-drag sequence, but whose redo method does not redo the whole press-drag sequence -- simply restoring the cursor to where the drag started.

    TL;DR In any case, having a history item per drag event would be costly in terms of space and bandwidth: even if the drags were (as they should be) merged.
